UCSIM

Professional AR/VR Application Development in Unity

During college, I did Co-op/part-time work at The University of Cincinnati's Center for Simulations and Virtual Environments Research.

At UCSIM, I worked on mostly AR/VR projects in the Unity game engine. The purposes of these projects varied, including immersive storytelling, and research projects, and medical simulations.

UCSIM has a small development team, which meant I had the opportunity to work with many different elements of the development pipeline. During my time there I had been responsible for gameplay programming, level design, environment design, event sequencing, performance optimization, and more.
